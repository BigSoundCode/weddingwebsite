Things to Do

At the Fairmont Château Montebello

Enjoy the historic Fairmont Le Château Montebello, the world's largest log cabin:

• Indoor/Outdoor pools

• Tennis, basketball, badminton and squash courts

• Spa services

• Golf course

• Walking trails

• Bike rentals

In Montebello

Explore the charming village of Montebello:

• Parc Omega - Wildlife park featuring Canadian wildlife (15 min drive)

• Chocomotive - Artisanal chocolate shop in a historic train station

• Brasserie Les Brasseurs de Montebello - Local craft brewery

• Manoir Papineau National Historic Site - Historic manor house

• Fromagerie Montebello - Cheese shop

• Restaurant Le Bistro Montebello - We are told this is the best pizza around!
